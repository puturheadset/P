Put Your Headset On

About

Put Your Headset On is a space where what cannot be spoken becomes heard and seen.

This project explores the relationship between sound, silence, perception, and atmosphere. Each release is not only something to listen to — but something to feel.

It is a curated environment. A minimal space. A moment between noise and meaning.

Listen carefully. There is always something being said.
